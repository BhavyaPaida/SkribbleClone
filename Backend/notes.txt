UTILS FOLDER

A utility is a function that is stateless, reusable, and 
has no knowledge of your application's domain.

If you could copy a util file into a completely different
 project (a food delivery app, a banking app) and it would
  still work without changing a single line — it belongs 
  in utils/

MODELS FOLDER
Think of models/ as the folder that answers:
"What does my data look like?"

utils/   → what your app CAN DO     (functions, helpers, pure logic)
models/  → what your app KNOWS ABOUT (data structures, shape of objects)`
